How to Get Rich

Date: 2023-12-22. Last updated: 2023-12-22.

1. Seek Wealth, Not Money or Status
追尋財富, 而不是金錢和地位

2. Making Money Isn’t About Luck
掙錢和運氣無關

3. Make Luck Your Destiny
讓運氣變成你和命運

4. You Won’t Get Rich Renting Out Your Time
通過出租時間的方式,不太可能致富

5. Live Below Your Means for Freedom
通過量入爲出獲得自由

6. Give Society What It Doesn’t Know How to Get
爲社會創造它想要但是還不知道如何獲取的東西

7. The Internet Has Massively Broadened Career Possibilities
互聯網極大擴展了職業的可能性

8. Play Long-term Games With Long-term People
和具有長線思維的人玩長線遊戲

9. Pick Partners With Intelligence, Energy and Integrity
選擇聰明，精力充沛和正直的合夥人

10. Partner With Rational Optimists
和理性樂觀者爲伍

11. Arm Yourself With Specific Knowledge
用特長知識武裝自己

12. Specific Knowledge Is Highly Creative or Technical
特長知識具有高度創新和技術性

13. Learn to Sell, Learn to Build
學習銷售，學習制造

14. Read What You Love Until You Love to Read
從讀妳喜歡的書開始，壹直到妳喜歡上閱讀

15. The Foundations Are Math and Logic
基礎是數學和邏輯

16. There’s No Actual Skill Called “Business”
沒有所謂“商業”技能

17. Embrace Accountability to Get Leverage
通過勇于承擔風險來獲得杠杆

18. Take Accountability to Earn Equity
通過勇于承擔風險來贏得資産

19. Labor and Capital Are Old Leverage
勞動力和資本是老式杠杆

20. Product and Media are New Leverage
産品和媒體是新杠杆

21. Product Leverage is Egalitarian
産品杠杆人人平等

22. Pick a Business Model With Leverage
尋找壹個能夠利用杠杆的生意

23. Example: From Laborer to Entrepreneur
從勞工到企業家的案例

24. Judgment Is the Decisive Skill
判斷就是決斷技能

25. Set an Aspirational Hourly Rate
給自己設定壹個超高時薪

26. Work As hard As You Can
奮力工作

27. Be Too Busy to “Do Coffee”
妳應該足夠忙，沒有多少時間去社交

28. Keep Redefing What You Do
不斷重新定義妳的工作

29. Escape Competition Through Authenticity
通過做自己避開競爭

30. Play Stupid Games, Win Stupid Prizes
玩愚蠢的遊戲，贏愚蠢的獎勵

31. Eventually You Will Get What You Deserve
最終妳會獲得妳應得的

32. Reject Most Advice
拒絕大部分建議

33. A Calm Mind, a Fit Body, a House Full of Love
平和的內心，健康的身體和充滿愛的家庭

34. There Are No Get Rich Quick Schemes
致富沒有捷徑

35. Productize Yourself
將自己産品化
