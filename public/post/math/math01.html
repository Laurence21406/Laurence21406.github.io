二角和差公式:
\begin{flalign} & \sin(\alpha \pm \beta) = \sin\alpha \cdot \cos\beta \pm \cos\alpha \cdot \sin\beta \\ & \cos(\alpha \pm \beta) = \cos\alpha \cdot \cos\beta \mp \sin\alpha \cdot \sin\beta \\ & \tan(\alpha \pm \beta) = \frac{\tan\alpha \pm \tan\beta}{1 \mp \tan\alpha\tan\beta} \\ & \cot(\alpha \pm \beta) = \frac{\cot\alpha\cot\beta \mp 1}{\cot\beta \pm \cot\alpha} \\ \end{flalign} 二倍角公式:
\begin{flalign} & \sin2\alpha = 2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha \\ & \cos2\alpha = \cos^{2}\alpha - \sin^{2}\alpha = 2\cos^{2}\alpha - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^{2}\alpha \\ & \tan2\alpha = \frac{2\tan\alpha}{1-\tan^{2}\alpha} \\ & \cot2\alpha = \frac{1}{\tan2\alpha} = \frac{1-\tan^{2}\alpha}{2\tan\alpha} = \frac{\cot^{2} - 1}{2\cot\alpha} \\ \end{flalign} 转换公式:
$$\sin^{2}\alpha + \cos^{2}\alpha = 1$$ $$1 + \tan^{2}\alpha = \sec^{2}\alpha$$ $$1 + \cot^{2}\alpha = \csc^{2}\alpha$$ 积化和差公式:
$$\sin\alpha\cos\beta = \frac{\sin(\alpha+\beta) + \sin(\alpha-\beta)}{2}$$ $$\cos\alpha\sin\beta = \frac{\sin(\alpha+\beta) - \sin(\alpha-\beta)}{2}$$ $$\cos\alpha\cos\beta = \frac{\cos(\alpha+\beta) + \cos(\alpha-\beta)}{2}$$ $$\sin\alpha\sin\beta = \frac{\cos(\alpha-\beta) - \cos(\alpha+\beta)}{2}$$ 和差化积公式： $$\alpha = \frac{\alpha + \beta}{2} + \frac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$$ $$\beta = \frac{\alpha + \beta}{2} - \frac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$$ $$\sin\alpha + \sin\beta = 2\sin\frac{\alpha + \beta}{2} \cos\frac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$$ $$\sin\alpha - \sin\beta = 2\cos\frac{\alpha + \beta}{2} \sin\frac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$$ $$\cos\alpha + \cos\beta = 2\cos\frac{\alpha + \beta}{2} \cos\frac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$$ $$\cos\alpha - \cos\beta = -2\sin\frac{\alpha + \beta}{2} \sin\frac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$$ $$\tan \alpha + \tan \beta = \frac{\sin(\alpha + \beta)}{\cos\alpha \cos\beta}$$ $$\tan \alpha - \tan \beta = \frac{\sin(\alpha - \beta)}{\cos\alpha \cos\beta}$$ $$\cot\alpha + \cot\beta = \frac{\sin(\alpha + \beta)}{\sin \alpha \sin \beta}$$ $$\cot\alpha - \cot\beta = - \frac{\sin(\alpha - \beta)}{\sin \alpha \sin \beta}$$ $$\tan \alpha + \cot \beta = \frac{\cos(\alpha - \beta)}{\cos\alpha \sin \beta}$$ $$\tan \alpha - \cot \beta = -\frac{\cos(\alpha + \beta)}{\cos\alpha \sin \beta}$$ 辅助角公式: \begin{flalign} a\sin x + b\cos x &= \sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}}(\sin x \cdot \frac{a}{\sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}}} + \cos x \cdot \frac{b}{\sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}}}) \\ &= \sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}} (\sin x \cdot \cos \alpha + \cos x \cdot \sin \alpha) \\ &= \sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}} \sin (x + \alpha) \\ & \cos\alpha = \frac{a}{\sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}}} , \sin\alpha = \frac{b}{\sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}}}, -\pi \leq \alpha < \pi \\ & \tan\alpha = \frac{b}{a} \end{flalign} 三角恒等式的魔法六边形

指数

$$x^{1} = x$$ $$x^{0} = 1$$ $$x^{-1} = \frac{1}{x}$$ $$x^{m}x^{n} = x^{m+n}$$ $$\frac{x^{m}}{x^{n}} = x^{m-n}$$ $$(x^{m})^{n} = x^{mn}$$ $$(xy)^{n} = x^{n}y^{n}$$ $$(x/y)^{n} = x^{n}/y^{n}$$ $$ x^{n} x^{-n} = 1$$ $$x^{-n} = \frac{1}{x^{n}} $$ 分数指数，像1/n，的意思是 取 n次方根 $$x^{\frac{1}{n}} = \sqrt[n]{x}$$ $$x^{\frac{m}{n}} = \sqrt[n]{x^{m}} = (\sqrt[n]{x})^{m}$$
