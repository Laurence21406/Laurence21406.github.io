极限

等价无穷小: x -> 0
$$ \sin x \sim x $$ $$ \tan x \sim x $$ $$ \arcsin x \sim x $$ $$ \arctan x \sim x $$ $$ e^{x} - 1 \sim x $$ $$ \ln(x+1) \sim x $$ $$ 1 - \cos x \sim \frac{1}{2}x^{2} $$ $$ (1 + \alpha x)^{\beta} - 1 \sim \alpha\beta x $$ $$ (1 + x)^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1 \sim \frac{1}{n}x $$ 重要公式: $$ \lim\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1 $$ $$ \lim\limits_{x \to 0} (1 + x)^{\frac{1}{x}} = e $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to \infty} \sqrt[n]{\alpha}(\alpha > 0) = 1 $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to \infty} \sqrt[n]{n} = 1 $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to +\infty} \arctan x = \frac{\pi}{2} $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to -\infty} \arctan x = -\frac{\pi}{2} $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to +\infty} \operatorname{arccot}{x} = 0 $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to -\infty} \operatorname{arccot}{x} = \pi $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to -\infty} e^{x} = 0 $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to +\infty} e^{x} = \infty $$ $$ \lim\limits_{n \to 0^{+}} x^{x} = 1 $$ 求极限方法总结
